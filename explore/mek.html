About this project

The Hungarian National corpus (referred to as Magyar Elektronikus Könyvtár, MEK) is the largest, freely available online Hungarian book collection.
It includes international classics like 1984 and Hungarian must-reads such as Köszivű ember Fiai.

Sadly, the website's design is rather cluttered making navigation and searching hard. Additionally, features, which would increase readablity, such as night mode, font change and other features available in E-Book readers are missing.
The aim of this software, is to re-render the UI of the MEK. It is fully compatible with any .html file that is on the original website.

In case of ideas, and found bugs, please contact AB@D.EU

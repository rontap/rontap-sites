Circle IO Tutorial
Your goal is to stop all of those pesky circles
You can do that simply by tapping a circle.
Beware, if you tap an inanimate circle, it will start moving again,
unbeliveable, eh?
Each time a circle collides with a wall, its counter increases, and its bad for us. This will be important later on.
Tutorial 1: How to survive

You can lose in 3 ways:
  • The sum of all collisions is too much (blue in the bottom part)
  • Have a single circle with too many collisons (green)
  • Run out of time (red)
    • Later, you will also be able to use powerups, to fight of these basterds.
    Tutorial 2: Bad news everyone

    These circles have come up with some serious technology!
    A new type of circle appeared, called collider
    You need multiple clicks before its timer runs out, or else
    it will generate a lot of collisions! Uh oh.
    Tutorial 3: Kill them before they lay eggs!

    Some Advanced circles have started laying... little halflings.
    The halflings (dotted border) dissapear instead of stopping, but they are smaller and faster.
    Focus the spawner down, as it will continue to lay eggs!
    Tutorial 4: Gotta Catch 'em all!

    Two new Advanced circles appeared!
    The Blocker has no timeout, and will block all powerups while not neutralised.
    The Runner has an average speed in the beginning, but keep an eye on it, as it gets progressively faster!
    Tutorial 5: I have a bad feeling about this

    They have warned me about the killer circle, and it looks that our time has come.
    The killer is extremely dangerous, if its timer runs out, you are instantly going to die.
    Oh, also, it lays Halflings and Regular Circles every time it touches a wall... At least its as big as a barn.
    Tutorial 6: They are evolving!

    New day, new evolved circles.
    The negator is a tricky circle, it gets its energy from other circles; and is immune to all clicking, do not even try clicking them, as it only spawns halflings.
    If you stop all other circles, the negator will stop too.
    The Vanisher hides and unhides each time it touches a wall.
